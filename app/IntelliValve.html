SlWiki IntelliValve Instructions!

  1. Mode button until you have blue led on SET.

  1. Use on red or yellow arrow to get to first position. After you have the first position click on save button and red led light will flash.

  1. Depending on the first position it will only go either go to positive side not the negative. So let’s say your first position is 12 you cannot decrease the value 10 since it can only be positive which can be like 14 or above. When you are moving to positive side you will see red led lights flashing for all positives. After you have the position, All you have to do is now click on save button which will go to service automatically then change mode to auto.

  1. Once it’s in auto, It will go to the first position in order to test the position all you have to do is use hold down to red or yellow at same time which will rotate the position from first to second.
