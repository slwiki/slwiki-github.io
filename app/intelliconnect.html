Intelliconnect wiring

  • Panel wiring for 240 wire to L1 and L2 which are two hots  coming from external power source since there is no ground  on intelliconnect since it’s plastic which needs to be capped off or connect to external breaker panel.
  • Panel wiring for 120 L1 will be hot  coming from external power source and L2 will be neutral.
  • Wiring Pump and Intellichlor to Intelliconnect for 240 Line 1 and Line 2 will be hot voltage coming in and Load 1 coming from external power source and Load 2 will be sharing connection with pump and intellichlor.
  • Connecting lights will be Line 1  coming from external power source  and Load 1 will be voltage going out to light it self it will be using half side of the relay.

Some notes in general:

no light controller via intelliconnect control*

the pump and wire to breaker direct,

then u would bring 2 wires off the breaker and wire them line side to filter pump relay

then ur transformer for ur lights would wire in load side to that second relay
