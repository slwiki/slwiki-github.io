Description

Pentair's ScreenLogic Connect is a convenient interface for your pool and spa, designed specifically for the iPhone, iPod touch, iPad and now Apple Watch. Control your pool and spa from a local WiFi network, or control them remotely over the Internet. Download this free app and check out the interface on our live demo!

Pentair customers with IntelliTouch, EasyTouch, and EasyTouch PL4/PSL4 pool control systems that want to use their iPhone/iPad/iPod touch must purchase the ScreenLogic interface kit (PN 522104) available from your local pool equipment retailer.

Pentair Water Pool and Spa, Inc Web SiteScreenLogic Connect Support

What's New in Version 6.2

Added new Complication and Glance views for Apple Watch. It's amazing to control and view your pool info from your wrist...anywhere!

Time and Date sync (iPhone or iPad to IntelliTouch and EasyTouch system) is now turned OFF by default. Please note that if enabled on IntelliTouch systems, it will result in the filter pump turning off for approximately 15 seconds after the time and date have been set. Time and Date sync can be enabled in the Settings icon (scroll down the list for the ScreenLogic app entry). It is recommended that EasyTouch users enable this feature.

Hiding circuits has been fixed. Set the name for any circuit (such as Pool or Spa, High Temp or Low Temp) to [Not Used]. This will hide it from the interface.

Please report any problems to pentairscreenlogic@gmail.com

We are working hard to make this app better for you!

Customer Reviews

Annoyingly functional....sort of...
     
by MikeyJL70

So, this app will let you do a couple of things remotely, however, there are a lot of bugs that need to big fixes before I'd give it more than just two stars.

First, the integration. When using the app, unless you're constantly selecting or changing a setting, it will log you off. Secondly, when changing a setting such as the heater/heat pump on or off, it may flash leaving you to believe you made a change but about 20% of the time, no change was made. The only way to tell if it accepted the change is to log off and then back on and see what it says. Same goes for temperature changes or cleaning mode. Thirdly, deselecting or deleting a scheduled mode is not possible through the app and if you've accidentally started one, you're screwed. Lastly, if you change a mode, say turn on cleaning mode because you want to clean your pool for a bit knowing that it is schedule to turn it off, it won't - it ignores the schedule and you'll have to do it manually.

Please fix this app.

Buggy update <Now FIXED!!>
     
by NCGator82

<UPDATE> the new version 6.2 fixes the intermittent restarts I was having previously. Pentair was responsive and help in working with me to create an acceptable workaround and all that is now corrected in the newest version.

I really like the new scheduling feature. However, some bugs seem to have been introduced. My spa shuts off intermittently (2 separate pumps) and restarts. Tonight in less than 40 minutes this happened more than 12 times. Have seen the same phenomenon in my pool as well with features running. After a feature has been selected and is running properly, the pump shuts off completely then restarts in high before settling back in to the requested speed for that feature. Very odd behavior but actually making much of my investment unusable.
